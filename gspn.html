Global South Perspectives Network (GSPN)

A platform that amplified voices from the Global South on peace, climate, and governance through dialogue, co-authored outputs, and joint advocacy.

Overview

GSPN convened practitioners, scholars, and community leaders to surface priorities that should shape global decision-making.

Network nodes

Regional nodes coordinated outreach and identified emerging issues; partners contributed evidence and case studies.

Dialogue

Regular virtual and in-person dialogues examined climate justice, debt, food security, digital inclusion, and peacebuilding.

Statements

Joint notes and op-eds presented collective positions and practical recommendations.

Events

Workshops and roundtables were timed around UN processes and regional forums.
