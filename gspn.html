Global South Perspectives Network (GSPN)

Amplifying voices from the Global South on peace, climate, and governance through dialogue, co-authored outputs, and joint advocacy.

Overview

GSPN convenes practitioners, scholars, and community leaders to surface priorities that should shape global decision-making.

Network nodes

Regional nodes coordinate outreach and identify emerging issues; partners contribute evidence and case studies.

Dialogue

Regular virtual and in-person dialogues examine climate justice, debt, food security, digital inclusion, and peacebuilding.

Statements

Joint notes and op-eds present collective positions and practical recommendations.

Events

Workshops and roundtables timed around UN processes and regional forums.
