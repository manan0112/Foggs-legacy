Project CASA / M4CE

Climate and Social Action (CASA) and M4CE (Multi-level, Multi-actor, Multi-sector Climate Engagement) link mitigation, adaptation, and equity through community-led solutions.

Overview

CASA/M4CE connects city networks, civil society, and youth coalitions to accelerate fair climate action.

Climate action

Practical guidance on resilience financing, locally led adaptation, and emissions reduction pathways.

Social equity

Ensuring climate policies address livelihoods, health, and inclusion, especially for frontline communities.

Futures and foresight

Scenario building and foresight workshops to stress-test plans and highlight co-benefits.

Partners

Collaboration with municipal alliances, research institutes, and practitioner networks across regions.
