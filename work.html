Work and Initiatives

From its establishment in 2013 till the ceasing of its operation in early 2026, the Foundation for Global Governance and Sustainability (FOGGS) functioned as a think-and-do tank, advancing thinking, writing, exchanges and advocacy on key issues of global governance, sustainability and citizenship, with a special focus on the actual role and potential of the United Nations as central node of the multilateral system.

What we do

FOGGS organised collaborative projects and networks, and used dialogue platforms to advance a number of key strategic initiatives. Below is an overview of its major activity strands.

Strategic initiatives

  • The quest for a new Grand Narrative of HopeExploring the building blocks of a shared story linking human security, planetary sustainability, and shared values for a fair and inclusive globalisation.
  • Reforming the United Nations – The UN2100 InitiativeForesight and policy ideas for an effective, ethical, and people-centred UN.
  • Global Resilience Council (GRC)As a distinct part of the UN2100 initiative, strengthening global governance to respond to systemic risks and interconnected crises.
  • Rethinking education for the digital era in a polycrisis context – COVIDEA
  • Turning military assets to civilian use – M4CE

Dialogue platforms and networks

  • Peace Reflection Group of former UN system staff (PRG)Former UN officials reflecting on peacebuilding and multilateral cooperation
  • Global South Perspectives Network (GSPN)Amplifying perspectives on cooperation, development, and reform
  • FOGGS Papers
  • FOGGS Open Consultation MondaysThe final “incarnation” of a series of online dialogues on key issues of global governance and geopolitics, peace and security, climate action and sustainability
  • Katoikos.worldOnline magazine and dialogue platform launched in 2014
  • Global Citizen PodcastsInterviews on global governance and social change

Research and policy projects

  • Project DEMOCRATDemocratic governance and participation in global contexts… building also on COVIDEA
  • The CASA ProjectFollowing on the footsteps of the M4CE strategic initiative… How military capabilities can support disaster response and crisis management
  • SDGs in IndiaApplying the SDG framework, identifying structural challenges and opportunities
  • Hellenic Forum for Sustainable Development (ELFOVA)Promoting sustainable development policies and practices in Greece, in partnership with ELIAMEP
