Work and Initiatives

Since 2013, the Foundation for Global Governance and Sustainability (FOGGS) has worked as a think-and-do tank at the intersection of governance, peace, sustainability, and international cooperation.

What we do

FOGGS operates through policy research, dialogue platforms, collaborative initiatives, publications, and public engagement. Below is a concise view of the major strands.

Strategic initiatives

  • The Grand NarrativeExploring a shared global story linking human development, planetary sustainability, and common values
  • UN2100 InitiativeForesight and policy ideas for an effective, ethical, and people-centred UN
  • Global Resilience Council (GRC)Strengthening global governance to respond to systemic risks and crises

Dialogue platforms and networks

  • Peace and Resilience Group (PRG)Former UN officials reflecting on peacebuilding and multilateral cooperation
  • Global South Perspectives NetworkAmplifying perspectives on cooperation, development, and reform
  • FOGGS Open Consultation MondaysOnline dialogues on governance, geopolitics, peace, and climate
