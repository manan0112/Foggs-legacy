About the Foundation

FOGGS — Foundation for Global Governance and Sustainability — was a Public Benefit Foundation (Fondation d'utilité publique) registered under Belgian law. The registered legal entity will be formally dissolved in 2026.

FOGGS was governed by its statutes, as originally published in the Moniteur Belge on 26 August 2013 and subsequently modified in 2018, 2020, and 2021.

  • Statutes — original (26 August 2013)
  • Statute modifications — 31 July 2018
  • Statute amendment — 20 February 2020
  • Statute modification — June 2021
  • Statute modification — March 2023
