Peace Reflection Group (PRG)

Former UN system staff offered independent reflections, appeals, and practical recommendations on peace and human security.

Overview

PRG members drew on decades of field and headquarters experience to highlight solutions for conflict prevention, protection of civilians, and accountable multilateral action.

Former UN system staff

Members included political, humanitarian, human rights, and development professionals from across the UN system.

Forum

Regular exchanges examined live crises, institutional reforms, and avenues for principled multilateral engagement.

Outputs

  • Statement in support of the UN Secretary-General (2024)
  • Enhanced role for the UN in peace and human security (2023)
  • Statement on Israel-Gaza (2023)

Partners

Collaborations with peace research institutes, regional organisations, and civil society networks strengthened outreach and implementation.
