COVIDEA

A rapid-ideas initiative launched during the COVID-19 crisis to generate constructive proposals for global governance, resilience, and solidarity.

Overview

COVIDEA collected concise ideas from diverse contributors to inform debates on pandemic response and future-proof multilateralism.
