DEMOCRAT Project

Living labs and policy workstreams that strengthened democratic practice, civic participation, and resilient institutions.

Overview

DEMOCRAT convened practitioners, researchers, and local actors to prototype tools and frameworks that kept democracies responsive and inclusive.

  • Focus: trust in institutions, participatory governance, digital rights, and democratic innovation.
  • Approach: co-design with local partners, field testing, and open sharing of learnings.

Living labs

Multi-city pilots tested new participation methods, dialogue formats, and community monitoring tools.

  • Tallinn conference agenda (2024)

Participation

Citizen assemblies, deliberative surveys, and digital feedback loops anchored decisions in community priorities.

Civic tech

Tools for transparent budgeting, fact-checking, and issue tracking were co-developed with local organisations.

Partners

Academic partners, municipalities, and civil society labs collaborated on pilots and evaluation.
