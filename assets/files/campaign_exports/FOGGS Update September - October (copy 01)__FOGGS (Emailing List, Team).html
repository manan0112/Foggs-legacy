FOGGS UN Day event on 24 October 2022
In view of the myriad of problems that our world is facing -- from the climate crisis and the COVID-19 pandemic to food, energy, water, and jobs insecurity, increasing inequalities, disrespect for basic human rights and destructive new wars -- a shift from confrontation to cooperation with an emphasis on human resilience against external and self-inflicted threats is more urgent than ever. FOGGS has long advocated that and has proposed a Global Resilience Council as a "UN Security Council for non-military threats". Is this vision out of reach under the circumstances or is there hope still? What do people from around the world, especially the youth, think? Join us for an open discussion on the future of our world on this UN Day, 24 October 2022.
FOGGS event in New York
on 23 September 2022
FOGGS hosted an in-person, all-day event on Friday, 23 September 2022 in New York, on the sidelines of the UN General Assembly's high-level week. The event included two panel discussions, one on addressing multi-dimensional threats to human security and the need for global governance reform, and the other on the Russia-Ukraine war and what can be done about it. You can read the Event Report here.
An Open Letter by former UN Staff to the world leaders and the UNSG
An "Appeal to World Leaders and the UN Secretary-General" was issued by former UN system staff on the occasion of the high-level segment of the UN General Assembly taking place in New York last September. The 128 signatories urged the UN member state leaders and the UNSG to use this occasion to advance efforts to end the fighting and set in motion a negotiation process in the ongoing Russia - Ukraine conflict. The initiative was supported by FOGGS and the full open letter can be found here and published on Katoikos.world.
GRC Website now also in Russian
The webpage dedicated to the FOGGS signature proposal for a Global Resilience Council (GRC) is now available, in addition to English, French and Spanish, in Russian too. Share all versions to help us spread the word about the GRC further around the world!
Digital with Purpose
FOGGS Executive Board Member, Veerle Vandeweerd, and Executive Director, Georgios Kostakos, participated in the Digital with Purpose Global Summit 2022 convened in Lisbon, Portugal, on 27 September 2022. Dr. Vandeweerd chaired the session on "Delivering Solutions for Education in a Rapidly Changing Digital World", where Dr. Kostakos presented the FOGGS -P4TT COVIDEA initiative on rethinking education. COVIDEA member Stephen Downes also spoke at the session.
COVIDEA engages with the private sector through GeSI's Digital with Purpose (DwP) initiative helping to benchmark the impact that digital products and services have on advancing implementation of SDG 4-- Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all --  and as an enabler for attaining all other SDGs.
Updates from FOGGS Partners
COALITION FOR THE UN WE NEED (C4UN) - OCA Discussion Series
 
Each month C4UN co-hosts, with the Baha'i International Community, a hybrid, off-the-record discussion related to Our Common Agenda. The next OCA Discussion Series event will take place on 1 November 2022 with the theme "Tipping the Scales: Proposals for UN reform in a time of need." Ambassador Christopher Lu from the Permanent Mission of the United States to the United Nations is among the scheduled participants, as the meeting format involves diplomats and officials responding to a series of reform proposals from civil society experts. FOGGS is a member of C4UN and participates actively in the Coalition's work.
