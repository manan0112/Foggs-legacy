Zero Draft on the Future of Global Governance | Online Event with Global South Perspectives Network
Date: 1 March, 2024
Time: 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM New York | 3:00 PM – 5:00 PM Brussels

An initiative of the Global South Perspectives Network(GSPN), this online event will include in the first half a Global South take on the recently issued Zero Draft of the Pact for the Future by a senior South African Foreign Ministry official, as well as commentary from senior representatives of the GSPN co-convening organizations, other GSPN members and guests. The second half will consist of a student workshop that will dissect the Zero Draft of the Pact for the Future and the overall Summit of the Future (SOTF) process from a Global South youth perspective, with the possibility of acquiring a certificate of participation for those students who will get actively engaged.

Register here to secure your participation.
Project DEMOCRAT conference opens at Tallinn University
The two-day conference of the DEMOCRAT Project on "Strengthening Democracy through Education: Conceptual and Practical Perspectives" opened successfully today at Tallinn University, Tallinn, Estonia. During the conference, conceptual and practical perspectives, including innovative ideas, techniques and initiatives supporting responsible democratic citizenship through education for democracy will be discussed. These can include revised curricula, formal and non-formal education, teachers' training, schools collaborating with communities, youth work, digital tools and many other aspects of learning / education for democracy. See the conference agenda here. A detailed event report will be included in the next FOGGS Update.
