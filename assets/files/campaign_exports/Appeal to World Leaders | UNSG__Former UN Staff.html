Dear Colleague,

A number of former UN system staff members, who like you signed the 15 April 2022 letter to the UNSG on the war in Ukraine, have continued to meet as the Peacemaking Reflection Group (PRG), to follow developments in and exchange views on the Russia-Ukraine war, and to explore possible ways of having a positive impact in the spirit of the UN Charter.

Members of the PRG have now prepared an open letter entitled:
 
APPEAL TO WORLD LEADERS AND THE UN SECRETARY-GENERAL

On the Occasion of the General Debate of the 77th session of the UN General Assembly
by the former UN system staff members signing below
 
You can find the open letter here.

If you agree with the content of the open letter and would like to add your signature to it, please use this Signature Form until the end of the coming Tuesday, 6 September 2022. On Wednesday, 7 September 2022 the open letter will be submitted to one or more media so that it can be published before world leaders arrive in New York for the high-level segment / General Debate of the 77th session of the UN General Assembly.

Looking forward to receiving your support for this new former UN system staff initiative through your signature, so that the impact of the open letter / appeal can be maximized,

Thanks and best regards,

Georgios Kostakos
