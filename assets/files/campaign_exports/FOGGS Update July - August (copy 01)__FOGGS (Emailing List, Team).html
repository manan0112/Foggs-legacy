FOGGS event in New York
on 23 September 2022
FOGGS will be hosting an in-person, all-day event on Friday, 23 September 2022 in New York, on the sidelines of the UN General Assembly high-level week. The event will consist of three successive panels, one on addressing multi-dimensional threats to human security and the Global Resilience Council (GRC) proposal of FOGGS, the second on broader UN reform issues, and the third on the Russia-Ukraine war and what can be done about it. If you are in New York on 23 September and interested in attending this by-invitation-only event please contact us at info@foggs.org
FOGGS Submits UN Reform Proposals to UN
Responding to a call by the UN Secretary-General's High-level Advisory Board (HLAB) on Effective Multilateralism, FOGGS submitted two proposals entitled respectively: 
  • RE-OPEN THE GLOBAL GOVERNANCE DOORS Through a Series of Global Conferences to Advance the SDGs and the 2030 Agenda
  • A GLOBAL RESILIENCE COUNCIL (GRC) To effectively Address Non-Military Threats to Human Security 
We look forward to seeing our proposals reflected in the final HLAB recommendations expected in early 2023.
