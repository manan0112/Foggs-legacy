Marking UNGA’s 80th Session Opening

In this Katoikos.world editorial, we set out expectations for UNGA’s 80th session opening: clarity, courage, and renewed multilateralism. To what extent were those expectations met by the leaders who gathered in New York? Join us on 13th October to discuss that -- see right column.

FOGGS endorses Joint Appeal for the International Day for the Total Elimination of Nuclear Weapons 
Since 2013, the UN has marked 26 September as the International Day for the Total Elimination of Nuclear Weapons, reminding us of the urgent need to act. FOGGS joined this year's Joint Appeal, reaffirming our commitment to a nuclear-weapon-free world and calling for concrete steps before the UN’s centennial in 2045.
