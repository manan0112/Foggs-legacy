No Human Rights Day Celebrations in Gaza
On the occasion of Human Rights Day, 10 December 2023, FOGGS Executive Director Georgios Kostakos critiques the veto cast by the USA at the UN Security Council on 8 December 2023, that prevented the adoption of a resolution urging a humanitarian ceasefire in Gaza. The veto is deemed detrimental to both Gaza's population and sustainable peace prospects in the region, on the one hand, and the institutional integrity of the UN, on the other hand. A call is made for medium and small countries to collectively lead the global governance system.

Human Security and Resilience in South East Europe
FOGGS Executive Director Georgios Kostakos participated in a panel discussion on "Human Security and Resilience in South East Europe" that took place at the TOBB University Campus, Ankara, Türkiye. The event was organized by the Regional Cooperation Council (RCC) and the Economic Policy Research Foundation of Turkey (TEPAV). It was part of engaging think tanks and academia in efforts towards implementing the SEE 2030 Strategy and the SDGs in the SEE Region. Major priorities: a just green and digital transformation, a reduction in depopulation and a reduction in poverty and inequalities, as well as fostering holistic resilience (i.e. preparedness, prevention planning, response management and post-disaster recovery).

FOGGS joins MEGA
FOGGS has joined MEGA - Mobilizing an Earth Governance Alliance, as a co-sponsoring organization. It is a coalition of civil society organizations working in cooperation with like-minded governments and other stakeholders to strengthen environmental governance.
