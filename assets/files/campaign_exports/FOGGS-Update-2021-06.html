Launch of The Global Citizen podcast series
The Global Citizen is a series of interview-based podcasts that aims to shine the spotlight on the people engaged with some of the most fascinating goings-on in our world, from UN officials to indigenous storytellers, and from grassroots activists to policy wonks. Big change may come one small step at a time, or through a quantum leap. We are open to both of these possibilities and actively try to provide a hospitable platform for underrepresented voices that bravely pursue their vision, in spite of the odds.

UNFCCC turns 30
As the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) turns 30 years old, FOGGS President and former UNFCCC Deputy Executive Secretary Richard Kinley asks when will the governments "grow up" to do their part on implementation. He argues that radical action is needed to ensure global peaking of emissions and net zero-emission goals are achieved in time. Based on current plans, this will not happen...
FOGGS at the T7 Summit in Berlin
FOGGS Executive Director Georgios Kostakos actively participated in the Think7 Summit in Berlin on 23-24 May 2022, highlighting the need for a Global Resilience Council, as well as the importance for the West to rethink its development cooperation approach and engage on a more equal footing with the rest of the world. The T7 Summit Communiqué can be found here and includes a reference to the Global Resilience Council on page 10.
