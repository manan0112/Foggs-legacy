Roundtable Discussions on the Future of Multilateralism hosted by FOGGS
FOGGS hosted in early 2022 two roundtable discussions on the UN Secretary-General's Our Common Agenda report and the future of multilateralism. The first roundtable was held on 27 January under the Chatham House Rule with the participation of senior officials from Permanent Missions to the UN and think tanks. The discussion summary can be found here. A second closed roundtable was held on 18 February with representatives of non-state actors, including science organizations and think tanks, local authorities and the private sector. More details and thediscussion summary can be found here.
