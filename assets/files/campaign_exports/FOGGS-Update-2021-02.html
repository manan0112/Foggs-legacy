This month our update focuses on the latest production of Katoikos.world articles that address key global issues. Of course we continue our work on global governance reform, including the proposal for a Global Resilience Council, SDG implementation, COVIDEA and the Grand Narrative, on which we will brief you in future updates.
FOGGS' President on UNFCCC COP 26
The climate crisis requires concrete action and implementation of existing agreements and commitments, not another overhyped summit. Will COP 26 in Glasgow do its part for the former or pretend to be the latter? Read Richard Kinley's take on the issue @Katoikos.world
Vacci-nation
COVID-19 vaccine production and deployment has been a much debated global issue this month. A series of great articles on Katoikos and the latest episode of "The Director's Cut" titled "Global Disorganization and Vaccine Nationalism" deal with the various aspects of this issue.

Stories of Love and Hate - The Narratives that Shape Our Present and Future

Historias de amor y odio - Las narrativas que dan forma a nuestro presente y futuro
En estos tiempos de enormes cambios y de mucha incertidumbre, ¿Cuáles son las historias que nos contamos para dar sentido a nuestras vidas? ¿A nuestras sociedades? ¿Al mundo? ¿En qué direcciones nos llevan estas historias? ¿Es posible cambiar de historia – y de camino?

Para una discusión sobre estas y más preguntas, con Leonardo Garnier (Costa Rica), Francisco Goldman (EE.UU./México), Nina Pacari (Ecuador) y Yoriko Yasukawa, Vicepresidenta de FOGGS - únase a nosotros!

5-7 pm hora Ecuador/Eastern Standard Time

Artist of the Month: Piera Van de Wiel
Piera Van de Wiel is a British singer and song-writer, actress, producer and humanitarian activist. The topics she advocates most strongly for are sustainability, mental health, and the elimination of violence against women and girls. With her single "Used" Piera Van der Wiel raises awareness for the significant increase in domestic violence amidst the COVID-19 pandemic.
