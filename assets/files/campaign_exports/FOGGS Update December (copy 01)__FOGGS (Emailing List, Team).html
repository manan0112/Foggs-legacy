December 2022 Update: End-of-year Message, New Katoikos Articles, Support FOGGS and HAPPY HOLIDAYS!
Dear FOGGS Family and Friends,

Another year another series of catalytic FOGGS initiatives for global governance, global sustainability and global citizenship.

Our Global Resilience Council (GRC) proposal has been receiving increasing recognition among state and non-state actors, through a series of online and in-person events, papers, and a dedicated web page in four languages; we are grateful to the Global Challenges Foundation for their generous support that made all this possible. We are also involved in the broader UN and global governance reform conversation, as active members of the Coalition for the UN We Need (C4UN), the Academic Council on the UN System (ACUNS) and the Global Governance Innovation Network (GGIN) in cooperation with partners like the Global Governance Forum, Greycells and HumanizaCom.

Our COVIDEA initiative for rethinking education in the digital era of poly-crisis has been part of a successful Horizon Europe proposal led by the University of Barcelona and will be contributing to the implementation of the DEMOCRAT Project starting in March 2023. COVIDEA is also working closely with GeSI in promoting SDG4 on education in IT sector companies, as part of the Digital with Purpose (DwP) initiative.

Our Militaries for Civilian Emergencies (M4CE) Project is getting ready for a second phase of implementation, as part of a broader partnership with colleagues from the Environment and Development Resource Centre (EDRC), the University of Texas, and the Global Military Advisory Council on Climate Change (GMACCC).

We are working with former UN system staff / retired international civil servants through the Peacemaking Reflection Group (PRG)  to promote a more pro-active UN stance vis-à-vis the Russia-Ukraine war and beyond, while we are not forgetting the quest for a new narrative for globalization, a narrative of hope that can be shared via video games or the arts, in addition to words.

Katoikos.world has been bustling with new articles and we also now have The Global Citizen podcast that you can enjoy.

We could say quite a bit more but, as a think-and-do tank, we prefer to say less and do more. And more we will do, with your moral and if possible also material support, to make this world a better place, for present and future generations, through key transformative initiatives that we nimbly promote.

All best wishes to you and your loved ones for the approaching holiday season and the new calendar year ahead,

Richard Kinley, President
Georgios Kostakos, Executive Director
We are very grateful to all our supporters and well-wishers for your continued encouragement and support. Funding for big-picture causes like ours is in short supply.
