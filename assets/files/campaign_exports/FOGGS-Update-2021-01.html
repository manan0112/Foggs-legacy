Celebrating the International Day of Education with COVIDEA!

January 24 has been designated by the UN General Assembly as International Day of Education, to raise awareness for the meaning of education for development and peace.

FOGGS is proud to be the co-convener of COVIDEA the COVID Education Alliance that aspires to build on the opportunities new technologies offer, as well as on the latest advances in education and related sciences, to propel education systems into the future, thus ensuring the resilience and well-being of learners and societies around the world. 

As the initiative develops, we are happy to see how much this topic resonates with our partners and educators around the world.
