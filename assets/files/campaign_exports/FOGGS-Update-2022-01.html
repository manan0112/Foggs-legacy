Global Health Diplomacy and Cooperation Report
The Lancet COVID-19 Commission’s Global Health Diplomacy and Cooperation Task Force, recently published a report on Global Diplomacy and Cooperation in Pandemic Times: Lessons and Recommendations from COVID-19. The Task Force laid out (1) the core values that should guide stakeholders in preparing for the next pandemic; (2) the lessons learned from the COVID-19 pandemic; and (3) recommendations to strengthen government and diplomatic responses to pandemics. Richard Kinley, the FOGGS President is a member of the Task Force and the report also includes an annex written by FOGGS Executive Board member Harris Gleckman. Among the report’s recommendations is the establishment of an institutional mechanism such as the FOGGS-proposed UN Global Resilience Council. The Council would have decision-making authority for non-military global threats and bring together specialized multilateral agencies, international financial institutions, and government ministries to address global crises in a cross-sectoral manner.
FOGGS participation in T7 Inception Conference
FOGGS Executive Director Georgios Kostakos took an active part in the Think7 Inception Conference on 24-26 January 2022. Think7, aka T7, works to develop and propose research-based policy recommendations to support the German G7 2022 presidency. The T7 process is jointly coordinated by the Global Solutions Initiative and the German Development Institute / Deutsches Institut für Entwicklungspolitik (DIE), with FOGGS among the participating think tanks.
Enterprising education for fit-for-future results
On the occasion of the International Day of Education and as part of the Learning Planet Festival 2022, this hybrid event (@Amsterdam and the Internet) was held on 26 January 2022 to consider bringing creativity, social awareness, resilience, problem-solving and participation into education though entrepreneurial parenting and a refocused education system. The event was co-organized by Parents International and COVIDEA – The COVID Education Alliance, which FOGGS convenes jointly with the Platform for Transformative Technologies (P4TT).

FOGGS TEAM VACANCY : GRC Website Development Internship

Duration : 3 to 6 months

Our Foundation is entering a phase of intense activity with the rolling out of our Global Resilience Council (GRC) Project. To support our team on advocacy activities and public engagement, we are currently offering an internship to help with the development of the GRC website.

This internship is fully integrated into our GRC project team, offering the prospective intern exposure to the work of a global think-and-do tank engaged in rethinking globalization and the institutions and mechanisms of global governance. The GRC project itself is related to reform discussions at the United Nations system in connection to the Secretary-General’s Our Common Agenda report of September 2021.
