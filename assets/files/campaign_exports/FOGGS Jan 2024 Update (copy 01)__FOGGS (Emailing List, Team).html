FOGGS Update: January 2024
PRG Call to avert catastrophe in Gaza, Written Submission for UN Pact for the Future, Countdown to DEMOCRAT Project conference in Tallinn, Katoikos article on India’s 75th Republic Day, and more


PRG Call to avert catastrophe in Gaza
The Peacemaking Reflection Group of Former UN System Staff (PRG), which is supported by FOGGS, issued a call to world leaders for immediate follow up on UNSC Resolution 2720 and the provision of urgently-needed humanitarian assistance to the population of Gaza. The call has been signed by 100+ individuals and organisations. The pdf version of the Call can be accessed here, and the full list of signatories can be found here. To add your signature, use this online form. The Call was issued before the recent order by the International Court of Justice (ICJ) in response to the genocide case brought before it by South Africa against Israel but is fully compatible with it. See also relevant article entitled "Human cost of war and its global impact must be ended" by Buyelwa Sonjica, Chairperson of the Advisory Council of the Inclusive Society Institute, FOGGS' South African partner in the Global South Perspectives Network (GSPN).
FOGGS Written Submission for UN Pact for the Future
In response to a call to civil society stakeholders by the Co-facilitators of the UN Summit of the Future (SOTF) negotiation process, FOGGS provided at the end of 2023 a written submission with key reform proposals that we would like to see included in the SOTF outcome to be called “Pact for the Future”. The subsequently issued Zero Draft of the Pact for the Future repeats globally accepted positions, not yet venturing into more innovative proposals. FOGGS will continue to engage with the SOTF process and push for a Global Resilience Council and other elements of much-needed UN reform.
Not too late to register for the conference ”Strengthening Democracy through Education: Conceptual and Practical Perspectives”, 27-28 February, Tallinn University, Estonia. It is the mid-term conference of the EU-funded DEMOCRAT Project carried out by FOGGS with nine other partners. In the meantime, the project deliverables have been accumulating, as are other project outputs, like this article by Janette Hughes et al. on global digital citizenship.

While Indians celebrate Independence Day as the day of gaining freedom from British Colonialism, Republic Day marks the adoption of the Indian Constitution as a free nation.
