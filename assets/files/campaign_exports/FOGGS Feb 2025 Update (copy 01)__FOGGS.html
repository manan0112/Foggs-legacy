FOGGS at the UN Townhall on Financing for Development
On 7 February 2025, FOGGS Executive Director Georgios Kostakos joined online the Townhall convened by the Co-facilitators of the Outcome Document of the Fourth Financing for Development Conference (FfD4). Georgios urged a shift from demandeur approaches to self-empowerment, emphasizing human security, local economies, and nutritional self-sufficiency. He called for redirecting resources from armaments. He also advocated for a stronger UN Economic and Social Council in global economic governance.
