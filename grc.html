Global Resilience Council (GRC)

A proposal for a high-level, inclusive body that safeguards people and planet by stress-testing global risks, advising leaders, and convening rapid responses.

Overview

The GRC idea matured through FOGGS roundtables, UN High-Level Advisory Board engagement, and consultations with civil society. It couples scientific foresight with diplomatic pathways to deliver actionable recommendations.

  • Mission: strengthen multilateral resilience to systemic risks (climate, pandemics, conflict, digital disruption).
  • Composition concept: co-chairs from global regions, science-policy interface, youth participation, and independent experts.
  • Core functions: horizon scanning, risk dashboards, rapid advice to multilateral bodies, and transparency to the public.
