Katoikos.world

FOGGS-affiliated online magazine featuring opinion, analysis, and dialogue on global issues.

Overview

Katoikos.world is an online publication platform and dialogue space dedicated to global governance, international affairs, and the wider challenges facing contemporary societies.

The platform publishes analysis, commentary, and creative contributions on geopolitics, sustainability, peace and security, and global cooperation—fostering informed discussion among experts, practitioners, and citizens.

Background and Context

Launched in 2014 as Katoikos.eu by José-Luis Herrero and Georgios Kostakos, the initiative began as a community-creation project to engage citizens across Europe in broad discussions on European affairs.

From autumn 2018, the platform relaunched as Katoikos.world as a self-standing project of the Foundation for Global Governance and Sustainability (FOGGS), aligning with FOGGS’ mission to promote fair and inclusive globalization through better global governance and active global citizenship.

Activities

The platform’s core activity is publishing articles and commentary on contemporary international issues across formats such as:

  • Analytical articles on geopolitical developments and global governance challenges
  • Opinion pieces and reflective essays on international affairs and societal trends
  • Editorial commentary on current global events
  • Contributions from citizen correspondents and external writers
  • Creative and artistic contributions addressing social and political themes
  • Multimedia content, including audio and video discussions

Outcomes or Impact

Katoikos.world provided an accessible platform for dialogue on global governance and international affairs, blending analytical writing, opinion pieces, and creative contributions.

The initiative advanced informed global citizenship, debate on the future of globalization, and exploration of pathways toward more inclusive and effective global governance.
